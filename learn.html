Ellie Chan - Modern Applications of Technology • 9/10/2020
Ellie's presentation was on modern uses & applications of technology, which the topics varied from Artificial Intelligence (AI), machine learning, robots, virtual/augmented reality (VR/AR), and how these technologies integrate to our everyday lives. To end off, Ellie briefly touched on how cybersecurity is related to the advancements of the technology industry, and the growing job market resulting from this. Using videos, she provided in-depth explanation to each topics. Slides can be found here.

Bian Lee - Networking and Servers • 9/3/2020
First club meeting of the year! Bian discussed about applications of cybersecurity with brief overview of different topics such as operating systems (specifically Linux environments), ethical hacking, steganography & cryptography as well as the importance of cybersecurity in general. In depth explanation was given specifically on servers and networking, and how you can build a simple Apache server at home to host a website. Bian demonstrated connecting and controlling another device using the SSH protocol under the same network in which he used a laptop to SSH into Linux desktop, and browsing through files and directories.
