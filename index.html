Northwood High School Cybersecurity Club

Cyber attacks have become one of the greatest threats in the modern world with fast growing technology industry that presents new vulnerabilities leading to cybercrime and huge destruction in the cyberspace. Thus, there have been an increase in demand for cybersecurity in recent years, in order to protect data and ensure privacy. Learn to identify and secure known vulnerabilities on a virtual network and participate in nation-wide competitions (including CyberPatriot & National Cyber League)! Meetings are held vritually on zoom every Thursdays at 3:35.
