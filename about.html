Meet The Board

Brian Yu Co-President

Brian Yu is currently a Senior at Northwood Highschool and he founded the club with Sydney Duong in 2019. His interest in cybersecurity was created by his father who currently works in the cybersecurity sector. He enjoys the problem solving aspects of cybersecurity and enjoys participating in a variety of CTF with his friends. He looks forward to meeting new people and creating an interest in cybersecurity among the community.

Sydney Duong Co-President

Sydney Duong is a senior at Northwood High School. She is one of the co-founders of the club, with her goal to raise awareness and educate about cybersecurity in her community. Sydney has participated in various cybersecurity competitions and held and volunteered in different workshops geared around cybersecurity ever since she started learning about the topic herself in middle school. She also likes music, art, and swimming and wishes for a successful and productive year for Cybersecurity Club!

Annabel Yang Vice President

Annabel Yang is currently a senior at Northwood High School. She enjoys drawing, both traditionally and digitally, as well as programming. While she doesn’t have the most experience in Cybersecurity, participating in CyberPatriot and NCL helped grow her interest in the field and passion for the club.

Tiffany Sun Secretary

Tiffany Sun is an incoming senior at Northwood High School and is the secretary of Northwood’s Cybersecurity Club. She has participated in CyberPatriot and National Cyber League for two years and enjoys learning more about cybersecurity when she can as she finds it similar to detective work. She has also participated in Irvine Hacks, in which her team created an anti-malware program. In her free time, she likes to paint, draw, or do yoga.

Brad Yu Treasurer

Brad Yu is a sophomore who attends Northwood High, he likes playing chess. He has participated in NCL and other cyber security competitions. He joined the club because he wanted to learn more about the web and how to protect oneself from malware. He is currently the treasurer of the Cyber Security Club.

Ellie Chan Event Coordinator

Ellie Chan is currently a sophomore at Northwood High School. In her free time, she likes singing, watching anime, doing math, reading, and drawing. Her favorite subjects are math and science. Although she is relatively inexperienced in coding, she enjoys it and hopes to continue to learn more about computer science. She has been doing CyberPatriot since 6th grade.

Bian Lee Publicity

Bian Lee is currently a junior at Northwood High School. He is the developer of this website and as a software engineer and full-stack web developer, he has worked on many different fields of technology from open-source developments (check out his GitHub!) as well as collaboration works with cryptologist figures such as Marcus Wannner and Nox Populi, in attempts to solve the Cicada 3301 puzzles. Besides programming, he also enjoys being a musician and a SoundCloud rapper/producer.
