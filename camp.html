Hello, we are the Cybersecurity Club at Northwood Highschool and we will be hosting a free summer workshop this year on Zoom from July 11th to July 13th at 1pm to 5pm.

The camp will introduce students to cybersecurity with a focus on Capture the Flag competitions and their application in the cybersecurity field. We will teach a variety of categories including ciphers, password cracking, osint, and forensics.

CTFs are competitions where participants utilize a variety of skills to obtain a unique plaintext “flag” that proves that the challenge has been solved. They are an incredibly fun and interactive way to learn cybersecurity skills and we hope to create a lifelong interest in cybersecurity through this camp.
